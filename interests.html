Abigail Harrison

Interests

I am a keen musician, having played various brass instruments from the age of 12. I currently play trombone in a ska band, regularly performing gigs on the weekends. I have been a member of various brass bands since 2005, including Cinderford Brass Band and Yiewsley and West Drayton Brass Band. I also ran and conducted the Yiewsley and West Drayton Training Band for the young (and young at heart).

I have been a member of Girlguiding UK since 2000, as a Brownie and a Guide. I helped start a new Young Leader unit for ages 14 to 25 in 2007, and I participated in and helped run several large scale events in Gloucestershire. As an adult, I have opened up a Guide unit in my area, as a unit leader.

I love being eco-friendly, ensuring I'm recycling as much as possible, and driving only when I need to, in order to have as little impact on the environment as I can.

I have a newly discovered love of coding, and I'm learning new things every day in a variety of programming languages.
